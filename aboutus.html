Who we are

We are a limited liability company that provides IT Outsourcing and Consultancy services and solutions for organizations, businesses and institutions of any size. These services are highly managed and focused towards keeping your systems operational and available so that you can focus your efforts on the demands of growing your business, managing costs and increasing revenues.

How we work

Strategic Support for Small & Medium Enterprises.

We provide your business with our highest level of support consisting of a flat fee annual support agreement or an alternative hourly charge of IT support on a need basis. We often recommend to new small business clients that they use this option during an evaluation phase before they decide to engage us for an on-going relationship. New clients also use our hourly support model when they need immediate assistance. While this option is costlier per hour than our advance-pay options, and tends to be used mostly when there is an urgent intervention.


Support for Large Scale Industries

HSE policy

Henrochie Limited is committed to giving priority to health, safety and security of personnel by doing everything possible to prevent injury to personnel and damage to property at work sites, and to protect everyone from foreseeable work hazards at any our work sites.

This policy is guided by the following principles:

  • • Creating awareness for safety through training slogans etc
  • • Conformance with established safety rules and regulations
  • • Conducting HSE Audit
  • • Reduction of risks and hazards to health and environment to as low as reasonably practicable
  • • Continuous improvement of the HSE management system
